My Journey

I am most proud of the journey I've taken from a challenging background to building a strong educational foundation and a meaningful career path. Coming from a poor family where my parents were not educated, my first major achievement was becoming the school topper in 10th grade across four schools. That experience showed me that determination and hard work can overcome any circumstance.

I went on to complete my B.Tech in Engineering, fulfilling a long-term goal I had set for myself. When the Y2K crisis impacted campus placements, I adapted by developing strong teaching skills and working as a teacher. This strengthened my communication, patience, leadership, and confidence.

After moving to the United States, I worked as a cashier and customer service associate, applying those same communication and teaching skills to connect with customers and business contractors. Within one year, I earned both the Bravery & Courage Award and the Best Cashier Award, recognizing my work ethic and ability to learn new systems quickly.

My curiosity about technology grew while working with retail software systems daily. Wanting to understand how software works behind the scenes, I independently learned Python and SQL and am now actively developing skills in JavaScript and Java. This reflects my continuous learning mindset, adaptability, and commitment to a career in technology.
